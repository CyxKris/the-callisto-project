The Mini Set

The Callisto Mini Set is the perfect way to sample the best of Callisto's premium tea blends. This set contains three different types of tea: Green Tea, Black Tea, and Oolong Tea, each in a convenient mini size. The set is designed to allow you to experience the unique flavours and health benefits of each tea without committing to a larger quantity.

[TEA SET]

-- Contents

Black Comet Mini

The Black Comet is a bold and robust blend of tea leaves sourced from India and Africa. It is fully fermented, giving it a rich and full-bodied flavour that is perfect for those who enjoy a stronger tea. Black tea is known for its bold flavour and high caffeine content, making it and ideal choice for a morning pick-me-up.

Green Galaxy Mini

The Green Galaxy, included in the Mini Set, is a refreshing blend of high-quality tea leaves sourced from China and Japan. This unfermented tea is steamed and dried, preserving the natural antioxidants and other beneficial compounds found in the tea leaves. It has a delicate, refreshing flavour and a mild aroma that is perfect for those who appreciate a light and refreshing tea.

Oolong Eclipse Mini

The Oolong Eclipse is a premium blend of semi-fermented tea leaves sourced from the best tea gardens in China and Taiwan. It has a mild and refreshing taste that is both energizing and soothing, making it a perfect choice for tea enthusiasts who appreciate a delicate balance between green and black teas.

-- Conclusion

Each mini pack contains enough tea for 5 cups, making it easy to enjoy a variety of teas without the hassle of buying the larger quantities. The set is also perfect for gifting to friends and family who love tea or for taking on the go. It is a great way to experience the best of Callisto's premium tea blends. With a variety of flavours and health benefits to choose from, there is something for everyone in this set.
