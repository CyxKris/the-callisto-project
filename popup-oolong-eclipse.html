The Oolong Eclipse

The Oolong Eclipse is a premium blend of semi-fermented tea leaves sourced from the fines tea gardens of China and Taiwan. This tea has a warm golden colour with a smooth, full-bodied flavour and a sweet, floral aroma.

-- Health Benefits

In addition to its delicious flavour and aroma, Oolong tea is also known for its numerous health benefits. It is a natural source of caffeine, which can help to increase alertness and focus without the jittery side effects of coffee. It also contains compounds that can help to promote weight loss, improve heart health, boost brain function, enhance digestive health, and promote health skin.

-- How To Prepare

To enjoy the full flavour of Callisto's Oolong Eclipse, it is important to prepare it correctly. Here is a simple recipe to help you brew the perfect cup of tea:

- Bring fresh, filtered water to a boil. The water should be hot, not boiling

- Add one tea bag to a cup

- Pour the hot water over the tea bag

- Leave the tea to steep for 2-5 minutes, depending on your desired strength

- Remove the tea bag

- Add sweetener or milk, if desired, and enjoy!

-- Conclusion

Callisto's Oolong Eclipse is a premium, high-quality tea that is both delicious and healthy. It is a perfect choice for those who prefer a milder taste that black tea but a stronger flavour than green tea.
