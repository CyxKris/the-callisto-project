GET TO KNOW MORE ABOUT CALLISTO.

Callisto is a tea company based in Lagos, Nigeria. The company was founded in 2021 with a vision to offer high-quality, organic teas that promote health and wellness in the local community and beyond.

OUR STORY

Amina, the founder of Callisto, left her career in finance after discovering the health benefits of tea in a Lagos market. She traveled the world to study the art of tea-making and sourced the finest organic teas to create unique blends that promote health and wellness. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, Amina built a community of tea lovers through Callisto. Join the community and taste the beauty of tea.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide the finest tea blends, sourced from the best organic tea gardens around the world, to Nigerians. We are committed to providing teas that are not only delicious but also promote a healthy lifestyle. We are also committed to promoting sustainable tea farming practices and supporting the local tea-growing community.

MORE INFORMATION

In addition to offering high-quality teas, Callisto is committed to promoting tea culture and educating Nigerians on the benefits of tea. We organize tea-tasting events, tea parties, and tea workshops to promote tea culture and educate Nigerians on the various types of tea and their benefits.

OUR TEAS

We offer a wide range of teas, including black, green and oolong teas. We source our tea from organic tea gardens in Asia, Africa, and South America. Callisto's teas are packaged in biodegradable and recyclable materials to minimize the impact on the environment.
