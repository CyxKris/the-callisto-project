The Green Galaxy

The Green Galaxy is a premium blend of high-quality tea leaves sourced from the best tea gardens in China and Japan. This tea is made from unfermented tea leaves that are gently steamed and dried, which helps to preserve the natural antioxidants and other beneficial compounds found in the tea leaves.

-- Health Benefits

Green tea is a rich source of a particular type of antioxidant called catechins. These compounds help to protect the body against damage caused by free radicals, which can contribute to chronic diseases such as heart disease, cancer, and Alzheimer's disease.

-- How To Prepare

To enjoy the full flavour of Callisto's Green Galaxy, it is important to prepare it correctly. Here is a simple recipe to help you brew the perfect cup of tea:

- Bring fresh, filtered water to a boil. The water should be hot, not boiling

- Add one tea bag to a cup

- Pour the hot water over the tea bag

- Leave the tea to steep for 2-5 minutes, depending on your desired strength

- Remove the tea bag

- Add sweetener or milk, if desired, and enjoy!

-- Conclusion

Overall, the Green Galaxy is a premium, high-quality tea that is both delicious and healthy. It is a perfect choice for tea enthusiasts who desire a mild flavour that is packed with beneficial compounds.
